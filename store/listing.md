## Detailed Description
Fill-A-Form AI uses Claude AI to intelligently match your saved profile to any web form — job applications, tax filings, immigration forms, medical intake, and more.

**How it works**
1. Save your profile (name, address, contact info, work details) — encrypted and stored locally on your device.
2. Visit any form on the web.
3. Click "Fill with AI" or press the button in the popup — the extension fills every matching field instantly.

**Key Features**
- 🤖 AI-powered field matching — understands context, not just field names
- 🔒 Zero-knowledge encryption — your data is AES-256 encrypted before storage; never shared without your action
- 👤 Multiple profiles — switch between Work, Personal, Travel, or any custom profile
- 📋 Fill history — track every autofill with date, site, and fields filled
- ☁️ Optional backend sync — sync profiles and history across devices
- ⚡ React & Vue compatible — works on modern single-page apps
- 🆓 Free tier: 20 fills/month — Premium: unlimited

**Privacy & Security**
- All profile data is encrypted with AES-256-GCM using a key that never leaves your device
- Profile data is only sent to the Anthropic API (over HTTPS) during an autofill — never to any other server
- No account required for the free tier
- GDPR compliant by design

**Use Cases**
- Job applications (LinkedIn, Greenhouse, Lever, Workday)
- Immigration & visa forms
- Tax filing portals
- Medical intake forms
- University admissions
- Government services

**Pricing**
- Free: 20 fills/month
- Premium: $9.99/month — unlimited fills, backend sync, priority support
