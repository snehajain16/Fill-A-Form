Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Fill-A-Form AI ("we", "us") is a Chrome extension that helps you autofill web forms using AI. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Data We Collect

Profile Data (stored locally)

Personal information you enter into the extension (name, address, email, etc.) is encrypted with AES-256-GCM and stored exclusively in chrome.storage.local on your device. The encryption key is generated locally and never transmitted.

Fill History (stored locally)

We record the URL, page title, number of fields filled, and which profile was used for each autofill. This data is stored locally and only synced to our servers if you explicitly enable backend sync.

API Calls

When you trigger an autofill, the following data is sent to the Anthropic API (Claude) over HTTPS:

  • The form field labels and types on the current page
  • Your profile data (in plain text, over encrypted HTTPS)
  • The page title and hostname

This data is used solely to generate field-to-value suggestions. Anthropic's data handling is governed by Anthropic's Privacy Policy.

2. Backend Sync (Optional)

If you provide a backend URL and token in Settings, your profiles and fill history will be synced to that server. We do not operate a mandatory backend — any server you configure is under your control. If you use our hosted backend (api.fill-a-form.ai), data is transmitted over HTTPS and stored with encryption at rest.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

  • We do not collect analytics or telemetry
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • We do not use cookies or tracking pixels
  • We do not read form values you have already entered

4. Your Rights

You can delete all locally stored data at any time by clicking Settings → Clear All Data in the extension popup, or by removing the extension (which automatically clears chrome.storage.local).

For data stored on our hosted backend, email privacy@fill-a-form.ai to request deletion.

5. GDPR

If you are in the European Economic Area, you have rights including access, rectification, erasure, portability, and objection. Contact us at the email above to exercise these rights. Our legal basis for processing is your explicit consent (you choose to save your profile and trigger autofill).

6. Children

This extension is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

7. Changes

We may update this policy. We will notify users via the Chrome Web Store update notes for material changes.

8. Contact

Questions? Email privacy@fill-a-form.ai
